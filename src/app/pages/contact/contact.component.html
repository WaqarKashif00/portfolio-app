FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically range from 8-16 months, while commercial projects may take 18-36 months from design to completion.

What is your design process?

Our process includes initial consultation, concept development, design refinement, documentation, and construction support. We work collaboratively with clients throughout each phase.

Do you offer sustainable design options?

Absolutely! We specialize in sustainable architecture and can help you achieve LEED certification, energy efficiency, and environmentally responsible design goals.

What are your fees?

Fees vary based on project type, size, and complexity. We offer competitive rates and provide detailed proposals after the initial consultation. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you work outside of Texas?

Yes! While based in Texas, we take on projects nationwide. We're licensed and experienced in working across multiple states.

Can you help with renovations?

Yes, we offer comprehensive renovation and remodeling services for both residential and commercial properties, from minor updates to complete transformations.
